About US

Big Fish Restaurant is a seafood Restaurant. the website is representing the restaurant services and allow users to navigate different services and browse the menu, schedule a Reservation, a especial Event, or get most updated information about restaurant hours of operation.

Responsiveness

the website scale nicely to fit any browser size. It is also responsive to mobile devices such as cellphones and tables. which permit users to access the pages using different devices without sacrificing the quality of look and feels or loosing content..

Usability

The Website is very user friendly. When users hover over a link it change its color. it also change color when the liked is clicked to let the users know that they have already visited that page.

Content

• The web site is organized in a very simple way and intuitive to navigate, not too many links and navigation tab
• The web site is appealing to the target audience with theme and colors related to seafood, and ocean.

Design

Repetition: there is one color team (Blue and light blue) that is kept throughout the pages.
Contrast: The blue and light blue are major colors theme on the website representing the ocean and calm
Proximity: Navigation tabs are kept closed to each other and picture are kept to minimum to avoid clutter.
Alignment: Well aligned picture text. It is consistent throughout the pages.
